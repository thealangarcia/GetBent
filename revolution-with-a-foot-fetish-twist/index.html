Vincent Skinwell

Vinny Skinwell is a disgraced former weatherman, part-time barfly, and full-time menace to mainstream media. Armed with a gravel-throated growl, a bottomless bottle of bourbon, and the journalistic ethics of a raccoon in a dumpster, Vinny drags the carcass of modern news through the alley of satire and sets it on fire for warmth. Once banned from AM radio for “creative use of traffic updates,” he now broadcasts straight from his undisclosed bunker, slinging sarcasm and chain-smoking through the apocalypse like it owes him money. If Walter Cronkite and Hunter S. Thompson had an illegitimate child raised by conspiracy theorists—Vinny’d be the reason it needed therapy.
