This Weed Seltzer Might Be Illegal—Cheers!

Vincent Skinwell uncorks the intoxicating truth about America’s new favorite buzz: cannabis-infused beverages. He's pouring over a drink scene so shady it makes moonshine look like holy water.

Vincent drags us through the fizzing minefield of hemp seltzers, THC lemonades, and mislabeling so dumb it might as well come with a waiver. Want a high? Cool. Want your liver to stay intact? Maybe skip the gas station gummies. With insurance policies barely holding the industry’s pants up, Skinwell explains why a sip today could be a lawsuit tomorrow.

So crack one open, pray it’s not laced with regret, and toast to the twisted world of weed drinks. This is the cocktail you didn’t know you ordered—with a dash of chaos, a splash of truth, and a big ol’ lime wedge of legal WTF.
