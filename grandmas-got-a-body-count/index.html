Grandma’s Got a Body Count

Tonight, Vincent Skinwell sets his sights on Florida’s latest nominee for Grandma of the Year: Tracy Nyx, whose babysitting résumé now reads like a horror film. After slow-roasting one grandkid in a hot car and losing another to an aquatic oopsie, she’s finally catching five years in prison—and somehow the debate is still about whether grandparents should babysit. Vincent isn’t here for your think pieces. He’s here to hand out rage with a side of sarcasm and remind folks that kids aren’t casseroles and pools aren’t pacifiers. Buckle up for a roast that hits harder than Florida sun on a closed windshield.
