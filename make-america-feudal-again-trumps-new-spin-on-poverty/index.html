Make America Feudal Again? Trump’s New Spin on Poverty

Vincent Skinwell dives into the latest economic absurdity coming out of the Trump camp: a full-throttle pitch that poverty is patriotic. With the economy tanking and store shelves emptier than ever, Trump’s team insists this is all part of the plan. Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick dreams of generational factory work, while Treasury’s Scott Bessent shrugs off cheap goods like they’re luxury problems. Vincent calls BS—served with bourbon, sarcasm, and a reminder that the American dream isn’t meant to look like a medieval reboot. Tune in, pour one, and don’t let the suits gaslight your wallet.
