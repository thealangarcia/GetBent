Mocktails and Crimes Against Booze

Vincent Skinwell takes a Molotov cocktail to mocktail culture in this episode of Get Bent. From the Gen Z “sober curious” trend to bars pushing lavender-scented sadness in a glass, Vinny tears into the multi-billion dollar mocktail industry like it personally wronged him. If you’ve ever paid $12 for fizzy juice in a fancy can, prepare to be verbally spanked. No filters, no training wheels—just straight shots of sarcasm with a twist of rage. Drink up. Get Bent.
